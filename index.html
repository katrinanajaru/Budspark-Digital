FAQ

+

How is pricing structured for projects?

We provide transparent pricing based on scope, complexity, and timeline. After discovery, you receive a clear estimate with milestones and payment terms.

+

How long does a typical project take?

Depending on requirements, most projects are delivered in phases within 3 to 12 weeks. You receive regular updates throughout implementation.

+

Who owns the final code and design assets?

On final payment, ownership of approved deliverables is transferred to you as defined in the project agreement, including source code, design files, and documentation.

+

Do you provide maintenance and post-launch support?

Yes. We provide maintenance options for updates, bug fixes, optimization, security improvements, and new feature rollouts.

+

How do revisions and change requests work?

Revisions are handled within agreed delivery phases. If scope changes, we share an updated estimate and timeline before proceeding.
